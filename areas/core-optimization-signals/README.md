# Core Optimization and Signals

This domain connects continuous optimization with systems, transforms, and signal representations that are widely used in ML and vision workflows.

## Areas

- [Numerical Optimization](numerical-optimization/README.md)
- [Signals and Systems](signals-and-systems/README.md)

## Why This Domain Matters

Optimization explains how learning systems are trained, while signals and systems explain how structured data is represented, filtered, and transformed.
